Personal Resilience and the 5% Rule

Strategic Effort Allocation in Broken Systems

The Strategy of Minimal Resource Allocation

When operating within a fundamentally corrupt system that offers neither trust nor reciprocal support, one strategic response involves allocating minimal effort. I call this the "5% Rule."

I provide five percent of my full capacity to environments that have demonstrated they neither deserve nor can effectively utilize greater investment. The remaining ninety-five percent I reserve for endeavors with genuine potential, for people who have proven trustworthy, and for work that genuinely matters.

def allocate_effort(system_trust_level):
